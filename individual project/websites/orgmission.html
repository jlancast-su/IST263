Mission Statement:

Detox league is crafted to find the best competitive esport athletes. We wish for our athletes to be technically skillful and communicative athletes that want to conquer the crown of the season. Detox league is designed to coordinate teams to teach minds alike transferable skills and enjoy video games with their team and competitors in order to nurture a wholesome community.

Welcome to Detox league.

Eligibility Requirement:

  • Team Eligibility:

    • Teams are required to assign one captain for the team. The captain will be in charge of communicating with the detox leaders and team members. Captains are required to be submitted with team registration along with a roster of participating members. Team registration will also be accompanied by a team deposit in order to register for Detox Compeitions.

  • Captain Role:

    • A captain can register with Detox Leaders but being a minimum of Thirteen years of age. We encourage those of that age to become Captains themselves in order to build teamwork skills and allow our community to become very close and comforting.
